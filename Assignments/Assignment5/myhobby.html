Why fitness?

I've always been an athletic type since the day I was born. I played all kinds of sports growing up and basketball was my favorite. I never lifted weights until I was in college.

Being Goal-Oriented

Being in some form of fitness, there are always goals to reach. Goal-setting is great for your life and even helps with your career. Without setting a standard for yourself, you won't be successful.

What kind of fitness are there?

Fitness is a broad subject. Fitness can be running, doing bodyweight training only, lifting weights, playing sports. It's the preference for you. It should not be done something forceful.

What do I do?

During college, I do various things. From bodybuilding, powerlifting, calisthenics (body weight training), and in the summer, I play basketball.
